Neural-Net Trainer
Employed the Brain.js Library to create a machine
that learns to use contrasting text colors.

Artificial neural-networks are a replica of how biological dendrites, cell bodies, and axons learn. The machine neural-net is not programmed with task-specific rules, rather it gleams how to perform tasks by considering training examples.

This specific neural net (above) was trained to determine what contrasting type color was most visible for any random color. It placed its' choice in the "Guess Text" section. By doing multiple training sessions of button clicks (white or black) to indicate what was the most visible text, the artificial intelligence quickly mastered how to determine the solution.
